# Government Environmental Interventions & Action Plans

Official public agency recommendations for municipal pollution mitigation:

1. **Traffic Restrictions (Odd-Even Scheme)**:
   - When AQI exceeds 300, implement odd-even license plate regulations for passenger cars.
   - Restrict heavy diesel trucks from entering municipal limits between 6 AM and 10 PM.

2. **Dust Mitigation (Mist Cannons & Sprinklers)**:
   - Deploy mist cannons along arterial corridors to suppress road dust.
   - Sprinkling water is required twice daily on unpaved roads and active construction pathways.

3. **Industrial Limitations**:
   - Suspend operations of coal-fired units and casting foundries during 'Severe' AQI conditions (AQI > 400).
   - Enforce transition to piped natural gas (PNG) for industrial boilers.

4. **Construction Controls**:
   - Pause major construction and excavation works when AQI exceeds 300.
   - Install continuous particulate monitoring sensors at construction site perimeters.
