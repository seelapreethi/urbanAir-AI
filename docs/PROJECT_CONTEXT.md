# UrbanAir AI - Project Context

## Project Name

UrbanAir AI – Smart City Air Quality Intelligence Platform

## Tagline

From Monitoring Pollution to Preventing It.

---

# Problem Statement

Build an AI-powered Urban Air Quality Intelligence platform that combines AQI monitoring stations, weather forecasts, satellite imagery, traffic information, land-use data, and public datasets into one intelligent decision-support platform for city administrators.

The goal is not just monitoring pollution but helping authorities understand pollution sources, predict future AQI, recommend interventions, and protect citizens.

---

# Primary Users

- City Administrators
- Pollution Control Board
- Municipal Corporation
- Health Department
- Citizens

---

# Core Product Vision

The platform should answer:

- Where is pollution occurring?
- Why is it occurring?
- What will happen next?
- What action should authorities take?
- Who is affected?
